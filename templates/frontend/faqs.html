Shipping Method

What is international shipping?

International shipping is a means of transport used for conveying goods (mostly) using sea routes rather than roads (road transport) or air channels (air transport).

What is local shipping?

Local shipping service allows customers to buy your products online and have you deliver them straight to their doorstep. Local delivery serves as an alternative to shipping with a carrier, in-store shopping, or in-store pickup by offering you a way to deliver directly to customers.

What is same day delivery?

Same-day shipping means that a customer's order will ship out on the same day the order is placed. This can cut a day or two off delivery time versus retailers that wait to ship orders. Same-day delivery means the customer will receive their order on the same day it's placed. .
